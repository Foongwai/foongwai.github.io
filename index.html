meet foong wai

Award Winning Photographer

Foong Wai is an award winning photographer, specialising in street, travel, and landscape photography. It's her chill-out hobby, all that stalking through alleys, streets, looking like a proper sociopath. She usually devolves into a series of typical photographer's stances - taking quick and stealthy aim at people, being incorrigibly dissatisfied with her angles over tricky light conditions atop a bald hill, or meticulously tweaking, burning, and dodging in post-production to bring out the best in her landscapes. Things that normal people do not get.
